# Operational Metrics

This document outlines the core metrics and KPIs that power the HireNestOS dashboards and organizational observability.

## Executive KPIs
* Total Revenue/Placements
* Time-to-Fill (Average)
* Cost-per-Hire
* Open Requirements Pipeline Health

## Candidate Lifecycle
* Candidates Uploaded
* Candidates Parsed
* Candidates Mapped
* Candidates Submitted
* Candidates Shortlisted
* Candidates Interviewed
* Candidates Selected
* Candidates Onboarded

**Conversion Funnel Tracking Example:**
`100 Uploaded -> 40 Mapped -> 20 Submitted -> 8 Interviewed -> 3 Selected -> 2 Onboarded`
*This funnel forms the core of the Executive Dashboard.*

## Vendor KPIs
* Total Bench
* Profiles Submitted
* Interview Rate
* Selection Rate
* Placement Rate
*Useful for evaluating and comparing multiple staffing vendors.*

## Recruiter KPIs
* Candidates Reviewed
* Candidates Mapped
* Submissions Created
* Interviews Scheduled
* Placements Closed
*Used for calculating recruiter productivity scoring.*

## Client KPIs
* Open Requirements
* Matched Candidates
* Interview Pipeline
* Offer Pipeline

## AI KPIs (Quality Metrics)
* AI Match Generated
* AI Match Accepted
* AI Match Rejected
* AI Match Submitted
* AI Match Selected
* **AI Acceptance Rate**
*Crucial long-term signal for determining trust and accuracy of the AI.*

## Platform KPIs
* Page Load Times
* Parsing Throughput
* Active Users
* System Error Rates
