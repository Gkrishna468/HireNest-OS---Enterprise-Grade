# Executive Reporting

This document defines the structure and frequency of operational intelligence reports generated by HireNestOS for executives, acting as the primary pulse of the business.

## Automated Weekly Executive Report

Generated automatically every Monday morning.

**Contents:**
1. **Platform Health & Throughput:** Funnel metrics tracking total candidates added, parsed, matched, submitted, interviewed, and placed.
2. **AI Health & Efficacy:** Average match score, matches accepted vs. rejected, and the critical **AI Acceptance Rate**.
3. **Vendor Intelligence:** Rankings by volume of submissions, interviews, and final placements (Conversion %).
4. **Recruiter Intelligence:** Productivity metrics (Submissions, Interviews, Offers, Placements).
5. **Governance Integrity:** Ownership conflicts resolved, duplicate submissions prevented, and release audits passed.

## Executive Dashboard Definitions

### Platform Funnel
A visual representation of the talent acquisition pipeline, moving from top-of-funnel (Candidates Uploaded) to bottom-of-funnel (Placements).

### AI Dashboard
Tracks the long-term trustworthiness of the matching engine. It is highly dependent on how recruiters and hiring managers interact with generated matches.

### Ownership Dashboard
Translates the structural integrity of the `Ownership Vault` into business value. Tracks the exact number of disputes resolved computationally, saving manual mediation time.

### Performance Dashboards (Vendor & Recruiter)
Normalizes performance tracking across both internal (recruitment teams) and external (vendor partners) staffing resources.
