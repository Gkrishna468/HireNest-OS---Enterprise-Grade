# HN-011: Enterprise Execution Completion Criteria

The architecture is now **frozen**. No new dashboards, Offices, agent categories, or architectural layers will be built until the runtime is proven.

HN-011 is not measured by code completed, but by autonomous business execution. The milestone is Done when the following 10 criteria are met:

1. **Autonomous Office Execution**: Offices execute their assigned work without manual intervention.
2. **Enterprise Scheduler**: All business events and scheduled jobs pass through the Enterprise Scheduler (respecting priorities, SLAs, and business hours).
3. **AI COO Resolution**: The AI COO detects blocked workflows, SLA risks, and bottlenecks, and resolves common failures automatically.
4. **Cross-Office Automation**: Workflows execute end-to-end across multiple Offices (e.g., Client Office -> Recruitment Office -> Vendor Office -> Finance Office).
5. **Universal Telemetry**: Every execution produces complete telemetry (duration, outcome, cost, retries, confidence).
6. **Enterprise Memory Updates**: Every execution updates the centralized Enterprise Memory, ensuring learning is never lost or siloed.
7. **Founder Morning Brief**: An automated executive briefing is generated before business hours (priorities, revenue forecast, risks).
8. **Founder EOD Report**: An automated end-of-day business report is generated (placements, revenue, pending approvals).
9. **Intelligence Loops**: The Candidate, Vendor, and Client learning loops are fully operational and demonstrably improve future predictions.
10. **Full Day Autonomy**: The platform can operate an entire 09:00 - 18:00 business day with minimal human intervention.

Once these criteria are met, HireNestOS will have fully transitioned from an AI-assisted application to a goal-driven, autonomous Enterprise Operating System.
