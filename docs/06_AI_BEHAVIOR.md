# AI Behavior Document

## AI Matching Rules (Scoring Weights)
- **Skills Match**: 40%
- **Experience Relevance**: 25%
- **Location Constraints**: 15%
- **Domain Expertise**: 10%
- **Certifications / Education**: 10%

## AI Ranking & Ethical Rules
- **NEVER** rank, score, or filter based on Gender, Age, or Nationality.
- Remove personally identifiable information (PII) parsing bias.
- Ensure consistent output format.

## AI Explanation Format
Each AI match analysis must yield:
1. **Match Score**: 0-100%
2. **Strengths**: Bulleted justifications.
3. **Missing Skills**: Clear identification of gaps.
4. **Risk Factors**: E.g., short tenures, missing critical certs.
