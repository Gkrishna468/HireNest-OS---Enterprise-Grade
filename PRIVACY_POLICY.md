# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** Today

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to HireNestOS ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit the application, and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect several types of information from and about users of our App, including information:
- **Personal Information:** By which you may be personally identified, such as name, email address, telephone number.
- **Career Information:** Resumes, job descriptions, professional profiles, and matching preferences.
- **Technical Data:** Details of your visits to our App, including traffic data, IP addresses, and other communication data.

## 3. How We Use Your Information (GDPR & CCPA Aligned)
We process your personal information to:
- Provide, operate, and maintain our AI-driven staffing platform.
- Extract skills and map candidates for matching purposes (using stateless AI processing).
- Prevent abuse, verify vendor trust, and enforce platform governance.
- Notify you about changes to our terms or services.

*Note on AI:* We do not use your personally identifiable information (PII) to train public AI models. Any extraction or matching utilizes transient inference in isolated tenant scopes.

## 4. Data Security
We have implemented strict measures to secure your personal information:
- Tenant isolation boundaries preventing cross-contamination.
- Secure HTTP headers and encryption for data at rest and in transit.
- Stateless AI execution environments.

## 5. Your Data Rights
If you are located in the EU (GDPR) or California (CCPA), you have the right to:
- Access the personal information we hold about you.
- Request correction or deletion of your information.
- Withdraw your consent at any time.

## 6. Contact Us
For questions regarding this privacy policy or your data, please contact the administrative team for this HireNestOS deployment.
