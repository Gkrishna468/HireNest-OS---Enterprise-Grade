# HireNest OS - Autonomous Staffing Orchestration Infrastructure

## 1. Project Vision
**HireNest OS** is an **Autonomous Staffing Execution Network**. It is designed to interpret staffing behavior, predict outcomes, and orchestrate execution through a verified trust layer. It moves beyond standard ATS/CRM workflows into a "Compounding Intelligence Infrastructure" where reputation, execution speed, and financial governance converge.

## 2. Core Pillars of the Infrastructure
1.  **Autonomous Orchestration**: AI-driven requirement routing based on vendor trust, historical delivery, and skill density.
2.  **Verified Trust Layer**: A multi-layered reputation graph (Trust Score AAA-C) that influences payout rates, routing priority, and system access.
3.  **Execution Event Bus**: An immutable, event-first architecture that captures every operational pulse for real-time analytics and predictions.
4.  **Reputation & Risk Memory**: Systemic detection of fraud, recycled candidates, and ghosting patterns across the entire network.
5.  **Economic Governance Engine**: High-fidelity tracking of margin realization, revenue at risk, and split-commission hierarchies.

## 3. Module Ecosystem

### 🧠 Autonomous Execution HQ (The Brain)
- **Dynamic Routing Engine**: Recommending the best vendor nodes based on Trust Index and Closure Probability.
- **Operational Predictions**: AI-forecasts for Time-to-Fill, Ghosting Risk, and Margin Leakage.
- **SLA Escalation Matrix**: Autonomous intervention based on feedback latency and submission velocity.

### 🛡️ Verified Trust & Risk Network (Supply)
- **Trust Scores (AAA-C)**: Metrics-based reputation tracking (Response Rate, Quality %, Closure Ratio).
- **Risk Assessment Center**: AI-detected signals for duplicate profiles, fake requirements, and suspicious vendors.
- **Identity Protocol**: Global synchronization of Aadhaar, GST, and Official Email validations.

### 🤝 Strategic Deal Rooms (Execution)
- **Collaborative Workspaces**: Real-time context sharing between Clients, Vendors, and Recruiters.
- **Execution Timelines**: Live event-bus feeds for every deal room interaction.
- **Milestone Verification**: Immutable tracking of payment triggers and SLA compliance.

### 💰 Economic Intelligence Layer (Finance)
- **Margin Realization**: Tracking profit capture vs. estimated leakage.
- **Pipeline Enterprise Value (EV)**: Projecting revenue realization across the active execution pool.
- **Split Management**: Automated handling of referral economics and co-delivery payouts.

## 4. Visual & Technical Stack
- **Architecture**: Event-driven Full-stack (React 18 + Node/Express) with a server-side "Intelligence Proxy."
- **Aesthetic**: "Technical Brutalist" (Slate-900 / Indigo-500) with JetBrains Mono accents.
- **AI**: Google Gemini Pro for deep extraction, strategic reasoning, and risk prediction.
- **Persistence**: Firebase Firestore with Relational ABAC (Attribute-Based Access Control) and an immutable Execution Event Bus.
